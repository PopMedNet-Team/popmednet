This PCORnet Distributed Research Network (DRN) Query Tool and Portal is for use by authorized users. All use must be related to PCORnet activities, and users must clearly describe the purpose of their query and the intended use of the query results. Results from PCORnet DRN Query Tool and Portal are intended to facilitate collaboration and research activities and cannot be published without prior approval of the data partners. External recipients of results from this tool must agree to these Terms and Conditions, and all information shared must be marked CONFIDENTIAL and sent securely.

Use of the tool is monitored and audited. User privileges may be revoked at any time.
